PORTFOLIO

Ozair is a curious, detail-oriented professional who believes in continuous learning. He started off his career as a computer programmer and worked in Bell Labs. As computer science faculty member he has taught university students databases and object oriented programming. Ozair worked in the oil and gas industry extensively providing solution in geomatics and GIS -- Geographic Information Systems. He has a passion for designing and developing innovative process automation and workflow solutions in this domain. Ozair has also provided services in technical writing producing white papers, case studies, blog posts, conference papers, and technical reports. He reads extensively and enjoys creative writing and swimming.

Ozair has worked in the technology and engineering domain of the oil and gas industry as well as in academia. He provided GIS support to Geomatics, Conceptual Design, Drilling, Engineering, Exploration and Planning departments of E&P organizations. He established GIS in Al Dhafra Petroleum and was instrumental in helping streamline business operations using technical innovations through proposing and prototyping software.

Programming languages: C/C++, Python, Visual Basic, HTML, CSS, SQL, PL/SQL

Frameworks: Bootstrap, X/Motif

Databases: Oracle, Informix, MS Access

Operating Systems: Windows, Unix

Packages: ArcGIS, Erdas Imagine, MS Office, Google Docs

Tools: Git/GitHub
